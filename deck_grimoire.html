# Quick Start ## Setup (30 seconds) 1. Download `deck_grimoire.html` 2. Open it in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge 3. Done No install. No account. No internet required. --- ## First things to do **Browse your decks** Click any deck tab in the nav. Each deck page has a profile describing how it's used, a jump nav to skip to suits or temples, and clickable cards that open a detail modal. **Add personal card notes** Click any card to open its modal. There's a notes field at the bottom — type anything. It saves automatically to your browser. **Log your first spread** Go to Pull Tracker. Pick a template or build your own: 1. Choose a template from the grid, or click **+ Add Card** manually 2. Use **+ Add Section** to group cards (e.g. Personality / Timing / Appearance) 3. Select deck, card, orientation, and add notes per card 4. Add tags to categorize the spread 5. Write an overall synthesis at the bottom 6. Click **✦ Log This Spread** **Back up your data** Once you have spreads logged, use the **Export Data** button in the tracker. Save the JSON file somewhere safe (Google Drive, iCloud, etc.) alongside the HTML file. --- ## Tips - The nav bar is sticky — always visible while scrolling - The ↑ button appears in the bottom right after scrolling — click to go back to top - Use **⧉ Copy** on a card row to duplicate it for fast multi-card entry from the same deck - The **Card History Search** in the tracker finds every spread a specific card has appeared in - **Most Pulled Cards** shows your top 8 with upright/reversed breakdown - Pull history filters by deck, tag, and free text search — all at once
